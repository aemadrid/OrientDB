Who are the contributors?

Contributors are all the people that contribute in any way to the OrientDB development as code, tests, documentation or other.

They talk in a private Mailing List with all the other committers and contributors and are updated on changes in internal stuff like binary protocol. One time patch doesn't make you a contributor, but if you're developing a driver or you sent more patches then you are a candidate to figure in this list.
